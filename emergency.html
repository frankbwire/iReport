Emergency Contacts


If you live in Nairobi and you need to contact the police or hospitals for ambulance services or rescue mission, we have a list of important numbers to call.

As you are aware, criminals can attack you in the wee hours of the night, when everyone is a sleep, this is the time you need the police most. These emergency/hotline numbers will help you to get police services. Also, when you have a sick person and desperately need an ambulance, this list will help you as well.
