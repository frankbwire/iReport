What We Do

iReport is a government web application that gives road users the ability to quickly report a traffic incident which will enable the government to easily facilitate the following services in a timely manner:

  1. Provide safety to road users.
  2. Map areas prone to road accidents.
  3. Provide effective and quick response to road incidents.
  4. Generate proper communication between the government and road users.
